About OATH

OATH was founded in 2014. There was a need to do more to help the "invisible faces" in our area. The ultimate goal is to establish a facility where we can provide the necessary services in improving the quality of life. Our facilities will provide job training, life skills training, and most importantly help get those in dire need off the dangerous streets, out of the elements, and give people a sense of pride, sense of purpose, and most importantly self-worth.

We are currently providing showers several days a week, grooming services and clothing. We also provide consultations for additional services that may be needed.
